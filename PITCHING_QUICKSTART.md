# Client Pitching System - Quick Start

## What This Does
Helps you research potential clients and generate personalized pitch emails for your AI/software engineering services.

## How to Use

### Simple Workflow

1. **Tell me who you want to pitch:**
   > "I want to pitch Acme Corporation"

2. **I'll research them automatically:**
   - Gather company info
   - Identify pain points
   - Match to AI solutions
   - Create client profile

3. **I'll generate a personalized email:**
   - Custom subject line
   - Personalized opening
   - Specific pain points
   - Proposed solutions
   - Your credentials
   - Clear call to action

4. **Review and send:**
   - You review the draft
   - I send via Gmail when you're ready

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Give me context:**
   - "I want to pitch [Company] - they do [what they do]"
   - "I saw [Company] recently [news/event]"

2. **Specify if you know their pain points:**
   - "They mentioned struggling with [specific problem]"
   - "I noticed they're hiring for [role], suggesting [pain point]"

3. **Tell me about any connection:**
   - "I was referred by [person]"
   - "I met them at [event]"
   - "It's a cold outreach"

4. **Request adjustments:**
   - "Make it shorter"
   - "Focus more on ROI"
   - "Add more technical details"
   - "Remove the section about [X]"
